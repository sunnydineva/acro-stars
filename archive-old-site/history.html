The Gardev Story

1992 - The Beginning

Gardev Milk Products was founded by the Gardev family in the small town of Kermen. Starting with just 5 cows and a passion for quality dairy, Georgi and Maria Gardev began producing milk and yogurt using traditional recipes passed down from their grandparents.

1995 - First Expansion

After gaining popularity in the local community, Gardev expanded their herd to 15 cows and began producing cheese. Their white cheese quickly became a local favorite, known for its rich, creamy texture and authentic flavor.

2000 - Modern Facilities

With growing demand, Gardev invested in modern production facilities while maintaining traditional production methods. This allowed them to increase production while ensuring the highest quality standards.

2005 - Regional Recognition

Gardev products began to be distributed throughout the region, gaining recognition for their exceptional quality. The company won its first "Best Dairy Producer" award at the regional agricultural fair.

2010 - Organic Certification

Committed to sustainable farming, Gardev obtained organic certification for their farm and products. This marked a significant milestone in their journey toward environmentally responsible dairy production.

2015 - Product Diversification

Gardev expanded their product line to include a wider variety of dairy products, including specialty cheeses, flavored yogurts, and traditional Bulgarian ayran. Each new product maintained the company's commitment to quality and tradition.

2020 - Sustainable Initiatives

Gardev implemented solar panels on their farm buildings and adopted water conservation practices, further reducing their environmental footprint. They also began using eco-friendly packaging for all their products.

Today - A Family Legacy

Now in its second generation of family ownership, Gardev Milk Products continues to grow while staying true to its roots. With over 50 cows and a dedicated team of local employees, Gardev remains committed to producing the finest dairy products while supporting the Kermen community.

Our Enduring Values

Throughout our history, certain values have remained constant:

  • Commitment to quality without compromise
  • Respect for traditional methods and recipes
  • Care for our animals and the environment
  • Support for our local community
  • Innovation that enhances, rather than replaces, tradition

These values continue to guide us as we write the next chapter in the Gardev story.
