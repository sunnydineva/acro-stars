Meet Our Happy Cows

At Gardev, we believe that happy cows produce the best milk. Our herd of over 50 cows lives in the lush pastures surrounding Kermen, enjoying fresh air, clean water, and plenty of space to roam.

Our Cow Breeds

Holstein-Friesian

Known for their distinctive black and white markings, our Holstein-Friesian cows are excellent milk producers. They're gentle giants with friendly personalities and produce milk that's perfect for drinking and making yogurt.

Jersey

Our Jersey cows are smaller in size but produce milk with a higher butterfat content, making it ideal for butter and cheese production. They have beautiful fawn coloring and large, expressive eyes.

Brown Swiss

These light brown to dark chocolate colored cows are known for their docile temperament and longevity. Their milk has an excellent protein-to-fat ratio, perfect for cheese making.

Bulgarian Rhodope

A local breed adapted to the Bulgarian climate, our Rhodope cows are hardy and produce milk with unique flavor characteristics that give our traditional products their authentic taste.

How We Care for Our Cows

Pasture-Raised

Our cows spend most of their days grazing freely on our organic pastures. We practice rotational grazing to ensure the land remains healthy and sustainable.

Organic Feed

During winter months when grazing is limited, our cows are fed organic hay and feed grown on our own farm without pesticides or chemical fertilizers.

Regular Veterinary Care

Our cows receive regular check-ups from our veterinarian to ensure they remain healthy. We use preventative care practices rather than routine antibiotics.

Comfortable Housing

When not grazing, our cows rest in spacious, clean barns with comfortable bedding and plenty of fresh water.

Our Milking Process

We milk our cows twice daily using modern, gentle milking equipment that ensures both cow comfort and milk quality. The milk is immediately cooled and processed in our on-site facility, minimizing transportation time and preserving freshness.

Our milking parlor is kept meticulously clean, and we follow strict hygiene protocols to ensure the highest quality milk. From cow to bottle, our milk typically takes less than 24 hours – that's what we call fresh!

Come Meet Our Cows!

We welcome visitors to our farm to meet our cows and see our operation firsthand. Farm tours are available by appointment on Tuesdays and Thursdays. Children especially love meeting our friendly cows and calves!
