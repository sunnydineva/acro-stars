The Gardev Farm

Nestled in the rolling hills surrounding Kermen, our 120-acre farm is where the Gardev story begins. Here, we combine traditional farming wisdom with modern sustainable practices to create the perfect environment for our cows and our dairy production.

Our Farm Features

Lush Pastures

Over 80 acres of our farm is dedicated to organic pastures where our cows graze freely. We use rotational grazing techniques to ensure the land remains healthy and productive year after year.

Modern Dairy Facility

Our state-of-the-art dairy processing facility combines traditional methods with modern technology. Built in 2010 and upgraded in 2020, it allows us to process milk on-site, ensuring maximum freshness and quality control.

Comfortable Barns

Our spacious, well-ventilated barns provide comfortable shelter for our cows during extreme weather. The barns feature soft bedding, plenty of space, and easy access to fresh water and feed.

Organic Feed Production

We grow much of our own feed on 30 acres of cropland, using organic farming methods without synthetic pesticides or fertilizers. This ensures our cows receive the highest quality nutrition year-round.

Our Sustainability Practices

Solar Energy

In 2020, we installed solar panels on our barn and processing facility roofs. These now generate over 60% of our farm's electricity needs, reducing our carbon footprint significantly.

Water Conservation

We've implemented a comprehensive water management system that includes rainwater collection, water recycling for cleaning, and efficient irrigation techniques that reduce our water usage by 40% compared to conventional farms.

Natural Fertilizers

We use composted manure from our cows as natural fertilizer for our fields, creating a closed-loop system that reduces waste and improves soil health without chemical inputs.

Biodiversity

We maintain hedgerows, wildflower meadows, and small wooded areas throughout the farm to support local wildlife and pollinators, creating a balanced ecosystem that benefits our farm and the surrounding environment.

From Farm to Table

At Gardev, we control every step of the production process:

  1. Milking: Our cows are milked twice daily in our clean, modern milking parlor.
  2. Processing: The milk is immediately cooled and processed in our on-site facility.
  3. Production: Depending on the product, the milk is pasteurized, cultured, or aged according to traditional recipes.
  4. Packaging: Products are packaged in eco-friendly materials that maintain freshness while minimizing environmental impact.
  5. Distribution: Our products are delivered to local stores and markets, typically within 24-48 hours of production.

This integrated approach allows us to maintain the highest standards of quality and freshness while minimizing our environmental footprint.
